Defination List

Mango
The mango fruit is roughly oval in shape, with an outer fleshy rind surrounding a stone. The flesh is soft and yellow-orange in color. The skin of the fruit is usually yellow-green to red.
Apple
The apple is a pome fruit, in which the fleshy fruit wall surrounds a seed-bearing core. When harvested, apples are usually roundish, 5–10 cm (2–4 inches) in diameter, and come shade of yellow, green, or red color. They vary in size, shape, and acidity depending on the variety.
Grapes
Grapes are fleshy, roundish fruits that grow in clusters made of many fruits of primary yellowish or greenish color. The pulp is juicy and sweet, and it contains several seeds or pips. It is a well-known fruit. It is eaten raw, although it is mainly used for making wine.
